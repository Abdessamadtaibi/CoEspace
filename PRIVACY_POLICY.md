# Privacy Policy for Co-Espace

**Last updated: February 01, 2026**

Co-Espace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Co-Espace.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

- **Contact Information**: Name, email address, and phone number required for account creation and management.
- **User Content**: Appointment details, patient notes, and scheduling data you input into the app.
- **Identifiers**: User IDs and device identifiers necessary for authentication and security.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use our app, including crash reports and performance data, to improve our services.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- To manage your account and authentication.
- To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support.
- To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We may use third-party services (such as authentication providers or analytics tools) that collect, monitor, and analyze this type of information in order to increase our app's functionality. These third-party service providers have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.
